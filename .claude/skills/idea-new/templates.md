# Document Templates

## 00-overview.md

```markdown
# {Idea Name}

> {One-line description}

**Created:** {YYYY-MM-DD}
**Status:** Draft

## The Problem

What pain point or inefficiency exists today? Who suffers from it?

## The Insight

What do you see that others don't? Why now?

## The Solution (High Level)

One paragraph describing what you'd build.

## Target Customer

Who is the first person that would pay for this? Be specific — not "businesses" but "solo e-commerce founders doing $10K-$100K/month who manually manage inventory across 3+ platforms."

## Why This Could Be Huge

What makes this a potentially disruptive, not incremental, business?
```
